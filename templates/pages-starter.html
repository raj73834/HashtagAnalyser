Tag, Total posts and Likes

In the Tag shows the keyword which are you want to search and analyse on it.
In Total posts display the total number of posts in the keyword which you have searched.
In the Likes if you get 200 posts from the instagram so Likes will represents the sum of 200 posts likes.

Related Hashtags

It will Generate top hashtags which are often used on your keyword. The hashtags can be different to the topic of the keyword, but they are popular hashtags with long range. The table will show ranking of a particular hashtag based on it's number of uses.
And also this thing is possible to show mentions(@) in the posts.

Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment is the attitude, thought, judgment prompted by feelings. It's effect (can be positive, negative or neutral) will be detected by a particular caption.

Likes & Views chart

It will represent likes of a paticular post and represents the trends of the searched hashtag.
You can easily track number of likes of a particular post done by a user.

Language Analysis

The chart will show different languages used for a particular post.

User frequency

It shows, how many times a particular hastag has been used by a user & the most popular hashtag mentions

User Analysis

The chart will show different From here you can see total number of likes, language, time of the particular post done by a particular user

Common Hashtags Counts

It shows, count of the most popular hashtags & the most popular hashtag mentions
